GTA: Kingdom is a brand new modification that is forked off of GTA: San Andreas. We will be presenting three brand-new, custom cities alongside the San Andreas classics: Los Santos, San Fierro and Las Venturas. We will also be introducing brand new characters and AI, new clothing options, new vehicles and weapons, and a brand new single-player and cooperative storyline mode (with up to 4 players at once) with split-screen and much more, all from a brand new launcher.
